How to play the game?

Game Overview

  1. You need to hit at least 100 points to win the game.
  2. Move your character around to collect gems to earn points.
  3. Green Gem = 10 pts, Blue Gem = 15 pts, Orange Gem = 20 pts.
  4. Be careful, if you get hit by a bug, you will lose 20 pts and return to starting point.

Game Control

  1. Use ← ↑ → ↓ to make your character move around.
  2. Use space button to pause the game at any time if your like.
  3. When you win the game (have more than 100 pts), press space to restart the game.
